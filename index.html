How Trailo moves you—not just your boxes

Every staircase, elevator, and parking spot changes the plan. We scope the job properly, put it in writing, and show up with the crew and equipment that match—so you are not guessing from a three-tier price grid on a website.

  1. Discovery

    You tell us both addresses, rough volume, stairs or elevator, tight carries, and anything fragile or oddly shaped. (437) 989-4480 or info@trailo.ca—whichever is easier.

  2. Walkthrough or video tour

    We size crew, truck, and time realistically. No “we will figure it out on the day” surprises that hit your invoice later.

  3. Written estimate

    Clear scope and assumptions. If access or inventory changes, we discuss it before we charge extra—not after the truck is loaded.

  4. Move day

    Floor protection, padded jambs, labeled rooms at the new place, and furniture placed where you want it before we close the truck doors.
